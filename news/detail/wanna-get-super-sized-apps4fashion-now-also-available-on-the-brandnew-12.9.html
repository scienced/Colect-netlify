Wanna get super sized?! Apps4Fashion now also available on the brandnew 12.9 inch iPad Pro

‹ back to news overview
Like us, Apple is also constantly innovating. To make your product presentations with Apps4Fashion even more impressive to your customers and keep pushing your sales orders, we kindly asked Tim (Cook) for a larger iPad. And here it is!
iPad has always offered a uniquely simple yet immersive experience. And now with its expansive 12.9-inch Retina display, nearly double the CPU performance of iPad Air 2, and refined Multi‑Touch technology, iPad Pro adds another dimension to that experience. It’s not just larger. It’s an iPad that lets you be more creative and more productive — at a whole new scale. Especially using Apps4Fashion in your showrooms all around the globe or integrated in your shop-in-shop concept to support your long-tail sales activities.

Apps4Fashion is the leading international mobile sales platform for Apparel, Sports, Footwear, Body Fashion, Accessory, Home & Living brands, agents and distributors. Currently over 300 international brands and suppliers like Scotch & Soda, Replay Jeans, By Malene Birger, Second Female, Lexington, O'Neill, J.Lindeberg, Peak Performance, Haglöfs, Asics, Björn Borg and many more are using our mobile B2B sales tools.

Do you also want to maximize your sales orders, reduce lost sale, increase efficiency of your sales force, save costs on samples and print work, and prove to be an innovative brand? Contact us for more information apps4fashion.com or email to sales@apptitude.nl
